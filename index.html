About Flash

Flash is a bot to help you with your server. It has more than 100+ commands that you can use for your server. Features such as moderation, welcome, fun, bump system, logging, utility, games, and much more!

Features

Security

Protect your server from unauthorized changes and malicious attacks with Flash's robust features, ensuring your community remains secure and stable.

Utility

Access a suite of helpful tools and commands, such as avatar, banner, userinfo, serverinfo, and many more functions, to enhance your server experience.

Moderation

Equip your moderators with powerful tools to manage your server effectively, including user management, message control, and customizable moderation actions.

Giveaway

Boost engagement with Flash, Our Giveaway command makes hosting and managing giveaways a breeze!

Welcome

Create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for new members with customizable welcome messages, role assignments, and introductory information to help them get started.

Ticket

Utilize the ticket system to manage support requests, report issues, or seek help from server staff in an organized and efficient manner.
